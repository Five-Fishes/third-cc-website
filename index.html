About Third CC

Third CC is a Chinese Community for students in Third Residential College of University Malaya (UM)

Third CC is a place for you to relax and make friends in you uni life. Third CC normally known as Home of Fish. All member of Third CC is act as a fish and Third CC is the home for all these fishes. This also indicate that Third CC is not just a community or society for the existing students who stay in Third Residential College of UM. It is a place for all fishes to join in anytime althought you are graduated from UM or not staying in Third College. Third CC is always welcome you for joining and have fun with us.

Our Mission

A platform for UM Chinese students to build bonding with each others and release stress

Activities

Previously Held 3rd CC Activities

中秋节

新生们会和学长姐一起去参与中秋节游行，有时还会相约喝茶。当然也少不了马大灯笼节提供的精彩表演及免费月饼。所接触到的马大灯笼节，也是很多人在马达所参与的第一个与中华文化相关的大型活动。

毕业典礼

毕业季，也是大学生涯最最少不了的一环。学弟妹将为学长姐们庆祝欢呼，祝福他们踏出校园后永远一帆风顺。

叁舍旅行

主要是为了让大家联络感情，在喘不过气的校园生活中，放慢脚步一起体验学习外的一切一切。

Food Recommendation

Here are some recommendation food for you around UM. Click on the food for navigation to the location. Enjoy your food.

*Images are just for reference

Name: Kim Seng Details: Chinese Food Restaurant at Seksyen 17. Famous with their cheaper mixed rice for student. Have other stir-fry food and noodle selling here.
Name: Nasi Kukus KK7 Details: Famous Nasi Kukus at KK7. Only RM5 and free add on rice. Open everyday including holidays. Always busy during lunch and dinner hours
Name: Seapark Nasi Lemak Bumbung Details: Nice supper street food near UM. Serve delicious nasi lemak and other mamak food. Services is fast, usually food is come within 10 mins after order. Located at Seapark.
Name: Chilli Pan Mee Details: Famous Pan Mee Restaurant at SS2. Having variety of pan mee at an affordable price.
Name: Boran Seapark Details: Classic Thai Food Restaurant. Thai Milk Tea and Thai Iced Coffee are serve here. Recommend for their delicious pork belly.
Name: Ribs King Details: Non halal food restaurant at Taman Paramount. Serve variety of delicious and juicy pork chop rice. Clean and comfortable place for your meal. Two branches are located at the area.
